## üìä CONFIDENCE RATINGS

### Color Code:
- **üü¢ Green (+6 to +10):** High confidence - strong evidence
- **üü° Yellow (+1 to +5):** Medium confidence - mixed evidence
- **üü† Orange (-5 to 0):** Low confidence - weak/selective evidence
- **üî¥ Red (-10 to -6):** Very low confidence - misleading/unreliable

### What Ratings Mean:
- **+10:** Peer-reviewed, transparent, comprehensive
- **+5:** Credible but incomplete or narrow scope
- **0:** Mixed quality, significant limitations
- **-5:** Selective presentation, missing context
- **-10:** Misleading, deceptive, or false claims
