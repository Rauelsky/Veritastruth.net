How Experts Think, Not Just What They Conclude

VERITAS shows the methodology behind truth assessments. We display multiple expert perspectives, transparent reasoning, and honest acknowledgment of what cannot be determined.

Our -10 to +10 confidence scale captures nuance. Validators explain their uncertainty factors, what evidence would change their assessment, and where legitimate disagreement exists.

"Cannot Determine" is a valid, respected answer. Epistemic humility builds trust.

Confidence Scale Example

+8 to +10: Very High Confidence
+5 to +7: Moderate Confidence
+2 to +4: Low Confidence
-1 to +1: Cannot Determine
-2 to -10: Confidence it's False
